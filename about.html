The Covenant of Care

Sunset Meadow was envisioned as a sanctuary where clinical precision meets the restorative power of a refined environment. We lead with a "Dignity First" mandate, ensuring every resident is celebrated within a framework of uncompromising medical safety.

Medical Excellence

Julianne Vance, RN

Specializing in 24/7 clinical oversight, Julianne bridges the gap between hospital-grade safety and the warmth of a true home. Her philosophy focuses on zero-deficiency standards and the implementation of on-site restorative therapy.

Executive Management

Eleanor Sterling

As Executive Director, Eleanor curates the Sunset Meadow atmosphere with an uncompromising eye for detail. Her leadership ensures that the "Boutique" experience is felt in every interaction, from concierge services to facility design.
